# Ford

Ford is an American multinational automaker that has been producing vehicles for over a century. Founded in 1903, the company has a long and storied history that includes the creation of iconic cars such as the Model T and the Mustang. Ford is known for its commitment to innovation and has been a pioneer in developing new technologies such as the assembly line and mass production methods. The company produces a wide range of vehicles, including cars, trucks, SUVs, and commercial vehicles, and is one of the world's largest automakers. With a focus on quality and reliability, Ford continues to be a popular choice for car buyers around the world.
