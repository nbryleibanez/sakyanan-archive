# Toyota

Toyota is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer that has become one of the world's largest automobile manufacturers. Known for its reliability and quality, Toyota produces a wide range of vehicles, from small economy cars to luxury SUVs and pickup trucks. Toyota's commitment to innovation and sustainability has also led to the development of hybrid and electric vehicles, making them a leader in environmentally-friendly transportation. With a global presence and a strong reputation for quality, Toyota continues to be a top choice for car buyers around the world.
