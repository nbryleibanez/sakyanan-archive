# Ferrari

Ferrari is an iconic Italian luxury sports car manufacturer that has become synonymous with speed, style, and performance. Founded in 1947 by Enzo Ferrari, the company has a rich racing heritage and has produced some of the world's most desirable and exclusive automobiles. Ferrari's cars are renowned for their sleek designs, powerful engines, and innovative technologies. The brand has also been a pioneer in developing advanced aerodynamics and lightweight materials, resulting in some of the fastest and most dynamic cars on the planet. Ferrari continues to captivate car enthusiasts and racing fans with its stunning vehicles and relentless pursuit of speed and innovation.
