# Mazda

Mazda is a Japanese automaker known for its stylish designs and driving dynamics. Founded in 1920, Mazda has grown into a globally recognized brand, producing a diverse range of vehicles including sedans, hatchbacks, SUVs, and sports cars. Mazda is known for its innovative engineering and has been recognized for its advancements in engine technology, including the SKYACTIV line of engines that provide impressive fuel efficiency without sacrificing performance. With a focus on creating a unique driving experience, Mazda has become a popular choice for drivers who value style, performance, and reliability.
