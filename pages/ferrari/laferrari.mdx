# La Ferrari

LaFerrari is a limited production hybrid supercar produced by the Italian sports car manufacturer Ferrari. It was first introduced in 2013 and only 499 units were produced.

LaFerrari features a hybrid powertrain that combines a 6.3-liter V12 engine with an electric motor, producing a combined power output of 950 horsepower. The car can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in less than 3 seconds and has a top speed of over 217 miles per hour.

The car's advanced technologies, including active aerodynamics, active suspension, and a sophisticated electronic stability control system, contribute to its exceptional handling and stability. The car's sleek design is aerodynamically optimized for maximum performance, with features such as a rear diffuser and active flaps that adjust to improve airflow.

The interior of LaFerrari is focused on performance, with a driver-centric layout that features a digital instrument cluster, a touchscreen infotainment system, and a minimalist design. The car is also equipped with advanced safety features such as airbags, traction control, and electronic stability control, ensuring the safety of the driver and passengers.

With its exceptional performance and advanced hybrid technology, LaFerrari is one of the most sought-after supercars in the world, representing the pinnacle of Ferrari's engineering and design capabilities.
