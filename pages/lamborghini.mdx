# Lamborghini

Lamborghini is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer known for its bold and striking designs, high-performance engines, and distinctive exhaust notes. Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, the company has created some of the world's most iconic and recognizable supercars. Lamborghini's cars are often noted for their aerodynamic shapes, sharp angles, and aggressive styling. The brand has also been at the forefront of innovation, with a focus on developing new technologies that enhance performance, handling, and driver experience. With a reputation for speed and style, Lamborghini continues to be a top choice for car enthusiasts who value luxury and performance.
